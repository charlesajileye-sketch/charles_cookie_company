Cookie flavours

  • Chocolate Chip
  • Crinkle Cookie
  • Peanut Butter
  • Snickerdoodle

Chocolate Chip

Soft or slightly crispy cookie with sweet dough filled with chocolate chips.

Crinkle Cookie

Soft, fudgy cookie with a cracked surface, usually coated in powdered sugar.

Peanut Butter

Rich, nutty cookie made with Peanut butter, often with a crisscross fork pattern.

Snickerdoodle

Soft, slightly tangy cookie rolled in cinnamon sugar.

Red Velvet

Soft cookie with a mild cocoa flavor and red color, often paired with cream cheese taste.
