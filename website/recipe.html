1. Chocolate Chip Cookie

The Chocolate Chip Cookie is a golden-brown treat with a soft, chewy center and crisp edges. It's filled with rich, melty chocolate chips and has a warm, buttery flavor that makes every bite comforting and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (melted and slightly cooled)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

2. Chocolate Crinkle Cookie

The Chocolate Crinkle Cookie is a soft, rich chocolate biscuit with a crackled surface coated in powdered sugar. It has a fudgy, brownie-like center and a slightly crisp exterior, making it both chewy and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • 120g all-purpose flour
  • 60g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 60g vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar for rolling:
    • White sugar
    • Brown sugar
